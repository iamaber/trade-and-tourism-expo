About the Expo

Positioning Bangladesh on the Global Tourism Map

The International Trade & Tourism Expo 2026 is Bangladesh's most ambitious tourism trade event — a carefully designed platform that brings the world's leading travel and hospitality stakeholders into direct engagement with South Asia's emerging market.

Hosted at Aloki, Tejgaon, Dhaka over two days in August 2026, the expo creates structured opportunities for B2B deal-making, brand positioning, and cross-border partnerships at a scale previously unseen in the region.
