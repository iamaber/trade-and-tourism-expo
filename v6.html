About the Expo

Where South Asia Meets
the Global Tourism Industry

The International Trade & Tourism Expo 2026 is Bangladesh's most prestigious gathering of global travel brands — a meticulously curated platform bringing together international tourism boards, airline carriers, luxury hotel groups, travel technology innovators, and diplomatic missions.

Held over two days at Aloki, Tejgaon, Dhaka, this inaugural edition establishes Bangladesh as a central node on the global tourism trade calendar, generating structured opportunities for B2B deal-making, brand positioning, and cross-border partnership at an unprecedented scale.
